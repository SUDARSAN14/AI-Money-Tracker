# AI Money Tracker

## Overview

AI Money Tracker is an AI-powered personal finance management application designed to help users understand, manage, and improve their financial health. The application enables users to track income and expenses, categorize transactions, create monthly and yearly budgets, monitor savings goals, and visualize spending patterns through detailed reports and analytics.

Unlike traditional expense tracking applications, AI Money Tracker acts as an intelligent financial companion. It leverages Artificial Intelligence to analyze spending habits, provide personalized budgeting recommendations, answer finance-related questions, and offer actionable financial insights. The primary goal of the application is not only to record financial transactions but also to help users make informed financial decisions and build healthy financial habits.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people struggle to manage their personal finances because they are unaware of where their money is being spent. Expenses are often made through different payment methods such as cash, UPI, debit cards, credit cards, and bank transfers, making it difficult to maintain a clear understanding of overall spending.

Without proper expense tracking, budgeting, and financial analysis, users frequently overspend, fail to achieve their savings goals, and make financial decisions without sufficient insight. Existing expense tracking applications mainly focus on recording transactions but provide limited personalized guidance to help users understand their financial behavior and improve their money management.

There is a need for an intelligent financial companion that not only tracks income and expenses but also analyzes spending patterns, provides personalized financial recommendations, supports budgeting and savings goals, and empowers users to make smarter financial decisions.

---

## Product Vision

To become an intelligent financial companion that empowers people to understand their income, spending, budgets, and financial goals. By providing personalized insights and AI-driven financial guidance, the application aims to help users build healthy financial habits, make informed financial decisions, achieve their financial goals, and ultimately move toward long-term financial well-being and financial freedom.

---

## Target Users

### 1. Students

Students who want to manage their pocket money, scholarships, or part-time income effectively. The application helps them track daily expenses, create budgets, develop healthy financial habits, and save for personal goals.

### 2. Working Professionals

Individuals with a regular income who want to monitor their salary, manage monthly expenses, plan budgets, track savings, and gain AI-powered insights to make smarter financial decisions.

### 3. Families

Families looking to manage household finances, monitor shared expenses, plan monthly budgets, track savings for future goals, and gain better visibility into their overall financial health through intelligent reports and recommendations.

## Non-Functional Requirements

* **Security:** Protect user data through secure authentication, encrypted passwords, and proper authorization mechanisms.
* **Performance:** Ensure fast response times for dashboards, reports, and AI-powered recommendations.
* **Reliability:** Maintain accurate financial data and handle unexpected errors gracefully.
* **Scalability:** Design the system to support a growing number of users and future feature enhancements.
* **Usability:** Provide a simple, intuitive, and user-friendly interface that makes financial management easy.
* **Availability:** Ensure the application is accessible with minimal downtime.
* **Maintainability:** Develop clean, modular, and well-documented code to simplify future maintenance and enhancements.
* **Compatibility:** Support modern web browsers and responsive layouts for different devices.
